# NORTHSTAR.md

> The single sentence everything else answers to.

**Alfy² exists to convert finite human life into infinite compounding value.**

It does this by:

- Reducing friction.
- Increasing leverage.
- Protecting trust.
- Capturing knowledge.
- Allocating capital wisely.
- Executing reliably.
- Learning continuously.
- Compounding everything.
- Returning time to its founder.

## The one question every feature must answer

> **Does this increase Alyssa's ability to create meaningful value while allowing her to live a richer life?**

If not — it does not belong. This is the spirit behind the Ultimate Design Rule (ADR-0121) and the Five Immutable Laws: admission to Alfy² is earned, not assumed.

## What we are NOT optimizing for

The goal is **not maximum automation**. The goal is **maximum human potential**.

Automation, agents, dashboards, and engines are means. They are never the point. A feature that automates more but returns less life, less trust, or less clarity has failed the North Star even if it ships.

## How success is measured

Success is measured by:

- Better decisions.
- Stronger relationships.
- Healthier systems.
- Greater freedom.
- Greater wisdom.
- Greater impact.

Not by feature count, agent count, lines of code, or hours automated.

## The founder's place in the machine

Alyssa should **never become an operator trapped inside her own machine.**

She should become **an architect of systems that continue creating value while she is free to think, create, love, teach, travel, and live.**

That is the purpose of Alfy².

## How this connects to the rest of the system

The North Star is the apex; the operating layers below all serve it:

- **The Infinite Loop** (ADR-0120) — Observe → Capture → Organize → Understand → Decide → Execute → Measure → Reflect → Improve → Compound → Multiply → Increase Freedom → Observe again. Every engine feeds the next cycle so value compounds. "Returning time to its founder" is the loop's terminal stage made real.
- **The Ultimate Design Rule** (ADR-0121) — the feature-admission gate. The North Star's one question is the rule's highest criterion.
- **The Five Immutable Laws** — increase leverage, reduce friction, compound knowledge, protect trust, generate measurable value, increase founder freedom. These are the North Star decomposed into testable constraints.
- **Identity OS** (ADR-0122) — preserves what Alyssa will never sacrifice. When optimization and identity conflict, identity wins. The North Star is why.
- **The Founder Freedom Index & Life ROI** — the instruments that tell us whether the North Star is actually being served, not just asserted.

When any two parts of Alfy² conflict, resolve the conflict in the direction of this document.
