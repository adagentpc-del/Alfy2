# 05 · Business Context

Alfie2 is built around Alyssa DelTorre (founder, strategic advisor, patent-pending AI architect) and a
portfolio of businesses. Each business is isolated (no cross-business contamination) and must make money
independently and repeatably with minimal manual pushing.

**Tier-1 business profiles (seeded in the `business-profile` engine):**
- `alfie2` — the platform itself (manages/improves the others; does not duplicate them).
- `move_mi` — moving/logistics platform; **first revenue slice** (Move Mi, pronounced "move me").
- `divini_procure` — developer/procurement bidding concept.
- `divini_partners` — partner/deal-flow concept.
- `stratalogic` — clinician-facing clinical decision support (compliance-sensitive; carries health
  disclaimers in its profile).

Each profile carries: identity, mission, offers/pricing, brand voice, **banned language**, compliance
caution, growth channels, source-of-truth systems, KPIs. See architecture §19 and the broader ecosystem
context in the founder's preferences. Revenue/incentive/pricing actions are always Alyssa-approval-gated.
