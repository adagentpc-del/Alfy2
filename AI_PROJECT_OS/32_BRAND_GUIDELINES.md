# 32 · Brand Guidelines

Alyssa's public authority sits at the intersection of AI-driven growth, founder intelligence, revenue
systems, strategic advisory, leadership psychology, human performance/longevity, high-stakes
negotiation, and patent-pending AI architecture.

**Voice:** direct, premium, strategic, high-conviction, emotionally intelligent, practical, slightly
provocative when useful. Not bland, timid, generic, or corporate. Avoid em-dash-heavy writing and
overusing "not this, but that" contrast lines. Clear headings, strong structure, conversion-aware.

**Public copy rule:** never reveal proprietary algorithm mechanics in public/investor-facing copy.
Each business carries its own brand voice + **banned language** + compliance caution in its
`business-profile` (see `05_BUSINESS_CONTEXT.md`).

> PLACEHOLDER: formal logo/typography/color brand kit not in-repo. Add assets + a style guide here when
> produced. The product design language is in `31_DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`.
