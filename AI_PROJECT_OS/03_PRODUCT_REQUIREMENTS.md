# 03 · Product Requirements

Requirements are maintained as the architecture + the prioritized build queue rather than a static PRD.

- **What the system must do / its layers:** `docs/ALFIE2_OPERATIONS_ARCHITECTURE.md` (Parts I & II:
  Mission Control, departments, AI org, chain of command, delegation, approval gates, context loading,
  review cadence, Revenue OS, FounderOS, Decision Engine, Capital Allocation, Continuous Improvement).
- **Prioritized, acceptance-criteria'd tasks:** `docs/ALFIE2_BUILD_QUEUE.md` (36 tasks, grouped).
- **Release scope:** `docs/ALFIE_RELEASE_PLAN.md` (R0–R10 with objectives/acceptance/risks/metrics).
- **Older PRD/spec drafts:** `docs/PRD.md`, `docs/TECH_SPEC.md`, `docs/BUILD_PLAN.md` (historical).

**Product philosophy (binding):** every feature must save time, make money, improve a decision, reduce
overwhelm/error, increase conversion/retention, improve safety/compliance, or create a durable asset —
else it does not ship. Every system answers: what changed, why it matters, what to do next.
